SPEECH TO SPEECH TRANSLATION SYSTEM FOR TRIBAL LANGUAGES

Speech is the fundamental mode of communication compared to other means for humans. The objective of the Project is to build a translator system which can translate spoken tribal speech to English speech and vice versa.

Objectives of the project are as follows:

  • Development of speech to speech translation framework for tribal languages of India
  • Development of speech to speech translation system for selected tribal languages in Kannada (Soliga and Lambani) and Odisha (Kui and Mudari)
  • Exploration of end-to-end solutions for speech to speech translation
  • Demonstration of applications (Digital Preservation, Health and Education Advisory, Voice Commerce and Animation Videos) using the developed S2ST system
